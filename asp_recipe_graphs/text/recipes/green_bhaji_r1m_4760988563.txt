title:
Green Bhaji

id: 4760988563

ingredients:
3 bunches spinach
2 -3 big onions
2 cups peas
8 -10 garlic flakes
1 inch ginger
8 green chilies

instructions:
Grind ginger, garlic, chillies, coriander to a fine paste adding very little water.
Heat ghee/oil add sliced onions & saute till brown.
Add the ground masala & fry on low heat till oil separates.
Add turmeric, salt, peas (cook it if ur using fresh ones), chopped spinach, chopped tomatoes.
Pour enough water, cover & cook in med.
Heat till tender.
Mix 1/2 tsp garam masala powder before removing from fire.
